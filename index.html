Here, we have combined the best of our ideas, taken into account how feasible or possible each idea is, and how we can explain each concept. We've also come up with a special extra-credit challenge #6 which draws on the students' imagination.


As a group, we combined our ideas and used the best of our imagination to find overarching concepts in all our sketches. This resulted in a robust final idea that could use a student's prerequisites to build smart security systems. Our main theme zeroed in to security cameras and imaging. We first designed the functionality (e.g. remote control and alarms) in challenge 1, and then moved to image processing and advanced MATLAB in later challenges. We have a CAD component for security camera design (PCB + 3D model). We also went the extra mile by involving OpenCV and Machine Learning using Python.
